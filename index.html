Our Craft

Artisans, Not Just Installers

Fernando Reyes came to Kauai in 2005 after training under master tile setters in Spain and Portugal — two countries with centuries of ceramic artisan tradition. He founded Pacific Tile & Stone in 2008 with a simple conviction: every floor, every wall, every surface deserves to be approached as a work of craft.

Today our team of six craftsmen brings that philosophy to Kauai homes and businesses — from plantation-era renovations in Lihue to oceanfront estate projects on Poipu's North Shore.
