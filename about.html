About Pacific Tile & Stone

Sixteen years of artisan tile and stone work on Kauai's Garden Island.

Our Story

Craft Rooted in Old-World Tradition

Fernando Reyes spent four years apprenticing under master tile setters in Seville and Lisbon — cities where ceramic artisan tradition dates back to the Moorish period. When he came to Kauai in 2005, he saw a market full of tile installers but very few genuine craftsmen.

He founded Pacific Tile & Stone in 2008, bringing European technical discipline to Hawaii's natural materials. The combination — old-world craft applied to volcanic basalt, Pacific reef stone, and tropical wood tile — has produced some of Kauai's most distinctive interiors.
